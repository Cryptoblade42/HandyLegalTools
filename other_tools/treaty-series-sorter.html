International Treaties Series Sorter

A tool to find the most authoritative international treaty series

Note that this is not a comprehensive sorter. However, r 8.4 of the AGLC4 makes it clear that, where possible, UNTS and LNTS are the preferred series (United Nations and League of Nations respectively). The next preference is for state party treaty series and then international/regional treaty series. There are many treaty series, so I have included only those listed in the AGLC4. If there is a treaty series you think I should include, let me know through the Contact page.

You must use the correct capitalisation for this sorter to work!
