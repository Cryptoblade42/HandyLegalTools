Welcome to Handy Legal Tools

A one-stop shop for all your legal study needs

Have you ever flicked through the AGLC4 and been simply overwhelmed by the number of rules? Have you ever been stumped trying to work out which law report series is the most authoritative? If you know exactly what I'm talking about, then this site was made for you. Here, you will find a collection of tools, the majority of which are citation generators for various sources, which you can use for your legal studies. It's free to use and there are no pesky ads to flood your screen. Do note that I have not 'enforced' any of the fields in the citation generator, so it is up to you to make sure you fill in everything correctly (including some formatting such as capitalisation)!

As a law/computer science student, I know how time-poor university studies can leave you so I hope the tools on this website can help you in your studies. If you have a suggestion or find any errors, please reach out using the details on the Contact page.
